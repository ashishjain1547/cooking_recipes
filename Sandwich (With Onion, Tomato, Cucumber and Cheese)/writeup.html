Ingredients:
1. Two tomato
2. Two onion
3. Cucumber
4. Cheese slices
5. Butter
6. Oregano
7. Chilli flakes

Steps

1. Chop the vegetables.






2. Take a slice of bread and spread butter on it.

3. Spread the mixture of chopped vegetables on the same slice of bread covered with butter.






4. Take another slice of bread and put the cheese slice on it.






5. Sprinkle black salt, oregano and chilli flakes on top of vegetables spread on the bread from step (3).

6. Put together the slices of bread (one with cheese and second with vegetables) to make a sandwich.

7. Heat the sandwich in a sandwich maker and your sandwich is ready.
